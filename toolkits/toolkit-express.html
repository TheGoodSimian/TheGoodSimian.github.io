The Learners Toolkit

    Expressing ignorance
        I don't know what X means
        I didn't understand what you said
        
    Asking for repetition
        Can you say that again?
        Can you repeat that?
        'Say again? <-- informal
        
    Asking for translation
        How do you say X in English?
        What's the English word for X?
        
    Asking by pointing to things (deixis)
        what do you call this/that?
        
    Asking by description
        What do you call 
            a person who...
            someone who...
            a thing which / which is... ?
    
    Asking for clarification
        What do you mean by that?
        What do you mean by X?
        Can you help me understand that?
